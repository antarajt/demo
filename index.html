First, you’ll need to generate a random image. By using a random image, you can challenge and surprise yourself by approaching your challenge from an unusual angle. There are many ways to generate a random image. One way to do it is by simply opening magazine and selecting the first image you see. What kind of image you use doesn’t really matter.
